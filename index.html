I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Aerospace and Mechanical Engineering at University of Notre Dame (2018 summer to 2023 summer) advised by Prof. Jian-Xun Wang. I am also very fortunate to work with Prof. Matthew J. Zahr. I received my master’s degree in Mechanical Engineering & Materials Science (MEMS) at Washington University in St. Louis (WUSTL) advised by Prof. Ramesh Agarwal and bachelor’s degree in Shanghai University of Electric Power.
